MRI Compatible Robotics

Enabling Technologies for Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)-Guided Prostate Interventions

(NIH funded project, approved by the Institutional Review Board (IRB) for clinical trials, in collaboration with Harvard Medical School, Johns Hopkins University, Queen’s University and Acoustic MedSystem, Inc.)
– Developed and debugged the controller hardware of 5-DOF robotic prostate biopsy system
– Implemented the kinematics model in robot controller with Java
– Evaluated the system accuracy with optical tracking system and MRI phantom studies
– Assessed the MRI compatibility of the robotic system in a 3T MRI scanner

MRI-Guided Robotic Assistant for Precision Conformal Ablation of Brain Tumors

(NIH funded project, in collaboration with Albany Medical College, UMass Medical School and Acoustic MedSystem, Inc.)
– Designed 8-DOF fully actuated robotic manipulator for brain tumor ablation
– Established and implemented the kinematics in robot controller
– Integrated system modules and tested clinical workflow

MRI-Guided Robotic System for Precision Deep Brain Stimulation Probe Placement

(In collaboration with Albany Medical College and UMass Medical School)
– Developed 6-DOF robotic manipulator for stereotactic neurosurgery
– Evaluated the system accuracy with optical tracking system and MRI phantom studies
– Assessed the MRI compatibility of the system in a 3T MRI scanner

Modeling of Asymmetric Tip Needle Steering

– Developed a novel needle steering model for asymmetric tip needle steering
– 2D model evaluation with camera tracking
– 3D model evaluation with CT phantom studies

Concentric Tube Continuum Robot with Piezoelectric Actuation for MRI-guided Intervention

(In collaboration with Vanderbilt University)
– Implemented the kinematics model of concentric tube continuum robot with MRI-compatible robot
– Evaluated the targeting accuracy with optical tracking system, MRI phantom studies, and MRI ex-vivo tissue studies

Fully Actuated Robotic Assistant for MRI-Guided Prostate Biopsy and Brachytherapy

(Congressionally Directed Medical Research Project (CDMRP) funded project, in collaboration with Harvard Medical School)
– Designed fully actuated 6-DOF robotic manipulator for prostate biopsy and brachytherapy
– Evaluated the targeting accuracy with MRI phantom studies

Teleoperation System with Hybrid Pneumatic-Piezoelectric Actuation for MRI-Guided Needle Insertion with Haptic Feedback

– Integrated Fabry-Perot interferometry (FPI) fiber optical force sensor with the slave robot
– Performed finite element analysis (FEA) and optimization of the sensor flexure on slave robot
